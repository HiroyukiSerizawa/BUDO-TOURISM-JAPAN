    <section id="etiquette" class="card">

      <div class="badge">ETIQUETTE｜礼法</div>

      <h2 class="lang ja">道場での基本マナー</h2>

      <h2 class="lang en hide">Dojo etiquette basics</h2>

      <ul class="list lang ja">

        <li>入退場時に一礼（<em>礼</em>）。</li>

        <li>畳の端に座り、先生・上級者の指示に従う。</li>

        <li>爪は短く、指輪や時計は外す。清潔な道衣（<em>道着</em>）を着用。</li>

        <li>写真や動画撮影は事前に許可を得る。</li>

      </ul>

      <ul class="list lang en hide">

        <li>Bow when entering/leaving the dojo (<em>rei</em>).</li>

        <li>Sit by the tatami edge; follow instructors and seniors.</li>

        <li>Keep nails short; remove jewelry; wear a clean <em>dogi/gi</em>.</li>

        <li>Always ask before filming or taking photos.</li>

      </ul>

      <p class="muted lang ja">※流派や施設により細部は異なります（例：合気会の案内・全日本剣道連盟の指針などを参照）。</p>

      <p class="muted lang en hide">Details vary by style and venue (see Aikikai guidance and AJKF concept/reiho notes).</p>

    </section>

      <h2 class="lang en hide">How to book a visit</h2>

      <ol class="list lang en hide">

        <li>Confirm requirements (membership, insurance, rank, etc.).</li>

        <li>Contact in advance (name, affiliation, years of practice, desired date).</li>

        <li>Arrive early; pay the fee; follow etiquette and enjoy practice.</li>
